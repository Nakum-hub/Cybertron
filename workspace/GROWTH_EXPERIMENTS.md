# Growth Experiment Backlog

## Experiment 1: CTA Copy Variant
- Hypothesis: sharper value proposition increases CTA clicks.
- Metric: `cta_click` rate.

## Experiment 2: Pricing Section Order
- Hypothesis: showing growth plan first improves conversion quality.
- Metric: pricing section engagement + auth initiation.

## Experiment 3: Trust Signal Placement
- Hypothesis: moving trust indicators above fold increases auth actions.
- Metric: `auth_action` initiation from landing page.

## Instrumentation Requirement
- All experiments must map to analytics events in `src/lib/analytics.ts`.
