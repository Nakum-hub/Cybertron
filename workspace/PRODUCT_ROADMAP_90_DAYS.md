# Product Roadmap (First 90 Days)

## Phase 1 (Days 1-30): Launch Foundation
- Finalize public landing narrative and core sections.
- Stabilize auth callback/error flow and backend integration baseline.
- Establish quality/release gates and role operating model.

## Phase 2 (Days 31-60): Conversion And Trust
- Add deeper pricing and comparison modules.
- Introduce customer proof blocks (use cases, testimonials, compliance posture).
- Integrate analytics dashboards for conversion and activation.

## Phase 3 (Days 61-90): Platform Expansion
- Add module shell for first secondary application.
- Add account workspace entry and tenant-aware navigation.
- Prepare design system v1 with reusable product-level components.

## North-Star Outcomes
- Reliable launch site with secure auth and telemetry-ready sections.
- Fast path from visitor to qualified lead / active user.
- Architecture ready for additional applications without redesign.
